Strength & Restince

The best way to shape your body and burn fat. Also improves your overall health while increasing muscle and bone strength. At the Tom's Gym we have two dedicated resistance zones located over two floors. Our large range of resistance equipment is guaranteed to meet your needs.

cardio

Improve general health & well-being with our large range of equipment. - Treadmills - Exercise Bikes - Concept 2 Rowers - Steppers - Crosstrainers - Arc Trainers

Spin

Simulates cycling on the road by using various sitting & standing positions whilst changing resistance via a flywheel. Great for Fitness, Strength & Weight Loss.

Swimming Pool

Whether you're looking for a relaxing swim or a venue for your swimming training, the swimming pool can accommodate you. Heated to 29C. our Pool is suitable for both Leisure & Rehabilitation purposes.

Personal Training

Reach your goals faster & stay motivated by working with one of our Tom's Gym Personal Trainers. Our experts offer support, advice and motivation to keep you on track.

Sauna

Relieve tension, relax muscles & re-energise. Regular usage has many proven health benefits: - Flush toxins - Cleanse the skin - Sleep better - Cardiovascular benefits
